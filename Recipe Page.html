Chicken Alfredo Pasta

This creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a simple and delicious recipe that anyone can make. Below you'll find the ingredients and step-by-step instructions.

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup cooked chicken pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and add the heavy cream.
  3. Add the parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce thickens.
  4. Mix in the cooked chicken and then combine with the pasta.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
